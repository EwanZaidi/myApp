Basketball games are actually the second largest sport in the world after the football game. However, the game is better known among the Chinese community in Malaysia.

However, basketball games have actually been ranked amongst other races in Malaysia as it is considered not a game for one race alone.

Basketball games have been contested among the Sekolah Berasrama Penuh (SBP) since 1974 by offering the Piala Tun Hamdan Sheikh Tahir for the men's category and Toh Puan Siti Zainab for the women category.
